QingMing VR Experience

About this project:

Qingming is a festival in which Chinese pay homage to their deceased loved ones, it is sometimes also called Tomb Sweeping Day and this celebration is held annually.


This VR experience was created to educate young adults about the traditions and procedures of Qingming and also allowing young adults from other races and cultures to experience how Qingming is like.


User can expect to experience the future of Qingming where we can send digital memories to the deceased as an offering.

I am the coder of this group. The software that i use to make all the scenes is unity.
